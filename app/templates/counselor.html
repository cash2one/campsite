About the Counselor Position

Counselors (ages 16-21) are the backbone of the entire Camp. Counselors manage and lead campers to ensure the campers are comfortable, cared for, and happy. Counselors assist with and teach many of the classes and activities during camp (such as chanting, philosophy, and clubs). Counselors are chosen on the basis of maturity, responsibility, and ability to function well in leadership roles. If you have any questions, please contact Aanand Parthasarathi at aanandp@gmail.com.

Criteria and Requirements

Counselors are selected based on the following criteria:
  • Counselor application essays and experience
  • Past performance as a camper or counselor (including any KYS experience)
  • Number of years attended as a camper and/or counselor
  • Age (prefer a mix of 16, 17 and 18+ year old counselors)
  • Special talents or skills. E.g. Knowledge of Hinduism, Dancing, Singing, etc.
  • Flexibility to attend either session
