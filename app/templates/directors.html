Session 2 Staff

Director | Gazal Kathuria

Gazal has been coming to camp since 2010. She's gone from camper to KYS member to counselor to head counselor. Last year she was fortunate enough to be Assistant Director and this year she feels blessed to be Director. Gazal is from Long Island, New York. She is a sophomore studying Management and International Business at the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University. Camp holds a huge place in Gazal's heart and has made her who she is today. She looks forward to working closely with the board and counselors and campers to make sure everyone has the best year at camp yet!

Director | Sandeep Chakravarthy

Sandeep has been coming to camp since 2005. He was a camper for 8 years, counselor, and Assistant Director for 2 years. This year, Sandeep is very excited to return as the Director of Second Session in 2017. He is currently a Sophomore at the University at Buffalo in Buffalo, New York. Camp has always been a second home to Sandeep. IT has given him a meaningful connection to his life and religion. It has given him a sense of purpose while he has made lifelong friendships. This year, Sandeep will strive to make camp as fun and memorable for everyone just as he has experienced years before.

Assistant Director | Vinay Simlot

Vinay has been coming to camp since 2007, spending time as a camper, counselor, head counselor, and most recently, Staff Coordinator. Vinay is from Sterling, Virginia, and is a freshman at the University of Colorado-Boulder, studying Aerospace Engineering. Over the past ten years, camp has become the place where Vinay can learn about his religion, while meeting people he will never forget. Vinay feels blessed to return as the Assistant Director, and has made it his personal mission to ensure every camper has the time of their life!

Assistant Director | Meghana Kuriya:

Meghana has been coming to camp since 2009. She has been a camper, counselor, staff coordinator and is thrilled to be coming back as assistant director. She is currently a freshman in Law School at the University of Ottawa. Her interests include politics, team sports, travelling. Camp means a lot to her, which is why she keeps coming back. Meghana aspires to make camp as great as it was for her when she was a camper.

Assistant Director | Nidhi Dontula

Nidhi has been coming to camp since 2007, spending seven years as a camper, two years as a counselor, one year as a head counselor, and is now very excited to be an assistant director. Nidhi is from Rochester, New York and is currently a freshman at Cornell University's School of Industrial & Labor Relations. Camp has had a profound impact on Nidhi through her lifelong friendships and experiences. Camp has allowed her to take on new responsibilities, learn about her heritage, and grow as an individual. Nidhi is so grateful for the opportunity to return this summer and give back to the place that she calls home. 

Assistant Director | Reema Kumar

Reema’s first year at camp was in 2009. Ever since then, camp has become a second home to her, and she has been coming back every year since. She has been a camper, counselor, head counselor, and is now assistant director. Reema currently attends Cornell University with a major in Statistics. Camp has always been a priority to her because the people she met here have taught her to appreciate her culture and religion and incorporate aspects of Hinduism into her daily life. Reema is excited to being coming back to camp and giving others the same experience she had!
