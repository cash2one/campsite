Facilities

Cabin: There are 6 cabins on the grounds. Campers and Counselors stay in the cabins. Campers and Counselors sleep on bunk beds. There are ventilation fans in the cabins to help with air circulation.

India Community Center (ICC): The ICC is an air conditioned building where campers spend much of their day: morning puja, chanting class, philosophy, meals, clubs and some evening programs. It is equipped with 3 classrooms, an infirmary, a large auditorium and a kitchen, where all the meals are prepared by volunteers.

Bathhouse: The bathhouse has separate boys and girls bathrooms. The bathhouses have toilet facilities, showers, sinks, and mirrors for the campers and counselors to use.

Camp Grounds: The camp grounds include two pavilions and two open fields where campers hang out and participate in sports and games every day.
