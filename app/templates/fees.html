Camp Fees

Camp Fee $1000.00
Fees include all activity costs including lodging, meals, and qualified supervision.
Sibling Discount $50 Discount
  • The sibling discount is applicable for 2nd child and additional campers from the same family.
  • The first child must pay the full camp fee.
  • The second child receives $50.00 off the full camp fee.
  • The third child receives $50.00 off the full camp fee.

    Payment Method
  • Hindu Heritage Summer Camp ONLY accepts checks. No Credit card payment is available.
  • We prefer payment in US currency. However, if payment is made in a foreign currency like Canadian, in addition to the fees, please add $30 per check to cover the bank fees
  • Checks must be made out to HHSC or Hindu Heritage Summer Camp Inc. and include the Camper Name and Session #
  • Please mail checks to
    • Hema Ganesan
    Treasurer, HHSC
    8 Tenbury Way
    Fairport, NY 14450

    Do not mail using USPS Certified Mail, UPS, Fed Ex, Signature Confirmation, or Overnight Mail. Send payment by First Class mail only.
    Transportation To $25

    HHSC can provide transportation from the airport/bus station/train station to the camp grounds. Please send the travel fees with the camp fee. All travel information must be provided to HHSC at least 2 weeks before camp.
    Transportation From $25

    HHSC can provide transportation from the camp grounds to the airport/bus station/train station. Please send the travel fees with the camp fee. All travel information must be provided to HHSC at least 2 weeks before camp.

    Registration is not complete until the camp fees are received and medical forms are submitted. Admission will be on a first come first serve basis depending on the camp capacity. We will confirm acceptance of your camper by email.
